How we met

We met in July, 2016 while at the URBN IT BBQ. Fawn was hanging out by the beer pong and Mike was talking with some friends nearby. We were introduced in passing by a co-worker. A few months later, Mike came to a Halloween party dressed as Luigi and came over to talk to Fawn and her friends. According to Fawn, the time they spent talking at that party felt as if time stood still and they were the only 2 people in the room. Eventually, Mike asked Fawn out for a date of drinks and pho. Fast-forward through many date nights with bonchon, old fashions, and surprise picnics by the Schuykill. We said I love you for the first time after the Eagles won the Superbowl while Fawn was visiting after having just moved to North Carolina. 2018 brought some changes as a job opportunity took Mike across the country. After a few months we decided to move in together in California, taking a drive with Vincenzo to see the country on the way. Ultimately, in November 2019, under a beautiful view of the Milky Way while camping in Mexico, Mike asked Fawn to marry him and now we all get to party!
